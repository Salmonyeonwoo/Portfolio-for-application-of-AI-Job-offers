AI Evolution: Agentic vs. Vertical AI

AI advances beyond simple information provision to attain expertise and execution capability.

Vertical AI (버티컬 AI)

Role: Domain-Specific Expert

  • Definition: Trained intensely only on specific industry knowledge (tourism, accommodation)
  • Strength: Maximizes accuracy and reliability compared to general AI
  • Application: Provides expert knowledge on complex fares/regulations, detailed room options, etc.

Agentic AI (에이전틱 AI)

Role: Autonomous Personal Assistant

  • Definition: Automatically performs complex, sequential actions based on a set goal
  • Strength: Executes real-world tasks through integration with external systems (booking, payment)
  • Application: Manages the entire travel process: planning, booking, modification, and cancellation
  • Cooperation: Uses Vertical AI's knowledge to execute actions
