Problem Definition: Safety Accident Risk

Potholes and road cracks cause vehicle damage and secondary accidents, threatening citizen safety.

Problem Definition: Slow Discovery and Processing

Reliance on citizen complaints and manual patrols leads to delayed discovery and slow response times.

Problem Definition: Budget and Personnel Inefficiency

Difficulty managing large areas with limited resources, leading to potential resource waste.

Proposed Idea: Data Collection

  • Installation of cameras on city buses, taxis, and cleaning vehicles.
  • Automatic recording of road video, GPS location, and capture time.

Proposed Idea: AI Model Utilization

  • Object Detection: Automatic identification of potholes, cracks, etc.
  • Risk Prediction: Classification of risk based on damage type, size, and location.

Proposed Idea: Administrator Dashboard

Web-based real-time road hazard monitoring system. Intuitive confirmation of risk level, damage type, and location on a map.

Proposed Idea: Citizen Participation App

A communication channel where citizens can film road hazards with their smartphones, report them, and check the real-time processing status.
