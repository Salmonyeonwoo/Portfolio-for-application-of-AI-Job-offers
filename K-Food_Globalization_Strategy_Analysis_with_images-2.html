The Role of 'Chef of the Tyrant'

K-Food Commonalities: Foundation of Success

1. Sincerity (Authenticity)

Emphasizes time and effort (e.g., Kimchi aging).

2. Hallyu Content Linkage

Powerful storytelling effect alongside K-Dramas, Movies.

Slide 3 / 9
K-Food Differentiating Factors

① Health Focus

Highlights the **probiotic** value of fermented products like Kimchi.

② Flavor Flexibility

Spiciness and umami create **new fusion menus** with local ingredients.

③ HMR Leadership

High-tech meal kits (Ramen, instant rice) maximize **accessibility**.

Slide 4 / 9
Differentiated Narrative: Image Innovation

Old Image: Static 'Fine Dining'

Low accessibility and high barriers to entry.

New Image: Dynamic 'Street Kitchen'

  • **Mobility:** Can be made anywhere.
  • **Localization:** Uses local ingredients.
  • **Approachability:** Feels like a dish 'made with a friend'.

Conclusion & Future Strategy

The future is not just exporting 'Korean cuisine', but providing a **'Global K-Food Experience'** fused with local culture.

Key: AI-Powered Localization & Digital Storytelling

Proposed Vision: 'Study Pony Campus'

A Free Virtual K-Food School empowering global users to adapt recipes with local ingredients, generating valuable localization data.
